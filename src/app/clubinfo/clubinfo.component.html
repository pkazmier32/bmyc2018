Welcome to the Bow Mar Yacht Club

Membership in the Bow Mar Yacht Club (BMYC) includes access to clubs boats such as Snipes, Lasers and Sunfish. Adult sailing lessons are also available.

You may use your BMYC membership card to gain admittance into any yacht club that is part of the Yachting Club of America. Go to Yachting Club of America

History

The Bow Mar Yacht Club was started in 1968
