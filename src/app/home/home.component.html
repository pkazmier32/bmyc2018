Club Info

The Bow Mar Yacht Club in Bow Mar, Colorado is open to members in good standing of the Bow Mar Owners Inc. BMYC supports active Snipe and Laser fleets that hold club races every weekend during the summer.

Juniors

The Bow Mar Yacht Club Juior Sailing program is a fantastic way to learn to sail and improve sailing skills. Junior sailing is for Bow May and Bow Mar South residents, ages 8 to 18 years old.
